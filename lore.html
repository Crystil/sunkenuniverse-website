The World After the Flood

Long before the islands, before the ruins, before the endless sea — there was a world. What remains now is fragmented history, half‑buried truth, and stories waiting to surface.

Timeline

What is known. What is believed. What is forbidden.

Before the Flood

Civilizations flourished across vast continents. Technology, magic, or power — something held the oceans at bay.

The Drowning

The seas rose faster than anyone could react. Cities vanished. Nations collapsed. History was erased overnight.

The Shattered Age

Survivors clung to mountaintops and artificial islands. Trade, piracy, and conflict defined the new world.

The Present

Ruins resurface. Artifacts are recovered. The truth of the Flood may not be as natural as believed.

Unanswered Questions

What caused the oceans to rise? Who benefited? And why do some ruins appear untouched by time? These mysteries are intentionally unresolved — to be discovered through play.
